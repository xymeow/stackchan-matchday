# Matchday MOD 1.5.0

[English](#english) | [简体中文](#简体中文)

## English

### Boss key: one-touch mute

- Hold the top touch bar for about one second to toggle mute; a balloon
  confirms the change while the finger is still down, and a persistent corner
  badge (`MUTE` / `静音`) shows whenever the robot is silenced.
- Mute suppresses everything disruptive in a meeting: remote-TTS speech, tone
  clips, raw `tone`, celebration motion, and alert light flashes. Silent
  information keeps flowing: probability bar, balloons, ticker, and the phone
  setup page are unaffected.
- `mute on [minutes]` / `mute off` / `mute status` over `POST /api/command`,
  a `{"action":"mute"}` JSON form, and control-panel buttons (including
  `mute 60m` for a timed meeting). Timed mutes announce "sound is back on"
  when they expire.
- An indefinite mute persists across reboots; timed mutes do not.

### Reliability

- Tone clips are skipped (successfully) while a TTS stream is playing instead
  of erroring into the watcher's retry path — one of the two halves of the
  mid-match retry-storm fix.
- The watcher now backs off 10 seconds before retrying a failed delivery and
  gives up on routine commentary after two attempts (goals, penalties, and
  red cards get five). A runaway retry loop could previously hammer the
  device into an out-of-memory reboot during live play.
- The screen no longer auto-dims while a probability bar or ticker is
  visible, removing the dim/wake flicker during quiet match stretches.

## 简体中文

### 老板键：一键静音

- 长按头顶触摸条约一秒即可切换静音；手指未松开时气泡就会确认，静音期间屏幕角落
  常驻 `静音` / `MUTE` 小标签，不会"神秘地没声音"。
- 静音抑制所有会打扰开会的输出：远程 TTS 语音、音效、`tone`、庆祝动作、警报闪灯。
  无声信息照常工作：概率条、气泡、滚动条和手机设置页不受影响。
- 支持 `mute on [分钟]` / `mute off` / `mute status` 命令、`{"action":"mute"}`
  JSON 形式与控制面板按钮（含"静音 60 分钟"定时模式）。定时静音到点后气泡提示
  "声音已恢复"。
- 不限时静音重启后保留；定时静音不跨重启。

### 可靠性

- TTS 播放中的音效改为成功跳过而不是报错，不再触发 watcher 的失败重试——
  这是比赛中"重试风暴"修复的设备侧一半。
- watcher 失败投递改为退避 10 秒后重试，普通实况最多重试 2 次（进球/点球/红牌
  放宽到 5 次）。此前失控的重试循环会在直播中把设备打到内存耗尽重启。
- 概率条或滚动条可见期间屏幕不再自动调暗，消除安静时段的暗/亮闪烁。
