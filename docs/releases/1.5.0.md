## English

### Watcher commentary personalization (watcher-only)

- Support and position are now separate narration perspectives. Routine play
  follows the supported team; major ESPN events and results add position
  benefit or pressure, including natural wording when support and position
  point in opposite directions.
- Match Setup enables the Kalshi market for the selected position team. With
  no position it defaults to the first market, but does not label that market
  as a holding. Only a confirmed matching `tracks_position` market adds
  position wording to price moves or suspected-goal alerts.
- The new global `config/espn_player_catalog.json` is keyed by ESPN athlete ID.
  Balanced/professional speech and balloons use formal names; casual speech may
  use a manually verified nickname. Legacy `player_names` and `star_chants`
  still override the catalog. Unknown players use ESPN's original name without
  an invented nickname.
- When a roster appears or changes, the watcher logs catalog coverage,
  featured-player count, and raw-name fallbacks for pre-match maintenance.
- These changes live entirely in the watcher. If Matchday Mod 1.5.0 is already
  installed, deploy the watcher code and `config/espn_player_catalog.json`,
  then restart the Python watcher process; do not reflash the mod or host.

### Boss key: one-touch mute

- Hold the top touch bar for about one second to toggle mute; a balloon
  confirms the change while the finger is still down, and a persistent corner
  badge (`MUTE` / `静音`) shows whenever the robot is silenced.
- Mute suppresses everything disruptive in a meeting: remote-TTS speech, tone
  clips, raw `tone`, celebration motion, and alert light flashes. Silent
  information keeps flowing: probability bar, balloons, ticker, and the phone
  setup page are unaffected.
- `mute on [minutes]` / `mute off` / `mute status` over `POST /api/command`,
  a `{"action":"mute"}` JSON form, and control-panel buttons (including
  `mute 60m` for a timed meeting). Timed mutes announce "sound is back on"
  when they expire.
- An indefinite mute persists across reboots; timed mutes do not.

### Reliability

- Goal and final-result feedback is now explicitly two-stage in the watcher:
  the local fanfare and head motion finish before dynamic LAN TTS starts. The
  TTS request remains blocking, so the watcher no longer opens four status
  connections per second while the WAV stream is active. A TTS-only failure
  does not replay an already delivered goal celebration.
- Suspected-goal market alerts restore the punchier "market jumped / possible
  goal / don't blink" cadence while still naming the inferred team and
  explicitly waiting for commentary confirmation.
- Tone clips are skipped (successfully) while a TTS stream is playing instead
  of erroring into the watcher's retry path — one of the two halves of the
  mid-match retry-storm fix.
- The watcher now backs off 10 seconds before retrying a failed delivery and
  gives up on routine commentary after two attempts (goals, penalties, and
  red cards get five). A runaway retry loop could previously hammer the
  device into an out-of-memory reboot during live play.
- The screen no longer auto-dims while a probability bar or ticker is
  visible, removing the dim/wake flicker during quiet match stretches.

### 老板键：一键静音

- 长按头顶触摸条约一秒即可切换静音；手指未松开时气泡就会确认，静音期间屏幕角落
  常驻 `静音` / `MUTE` 小标签，不会"神秘地没声音"。
- 静音抑制所有会打扰开会的输出：远程 TTS 语音、音效、`tone`、庆祝动作、警报闪灯。
  无声信息照常工作：概率条、气泡、滚动条和手机设置页不受影响。
- 支持 `mute on [分钟]` / `mute off` / `mute status` 命令、`{"action":"mute"}`
  JSON 形式与控制面板按钮（含"静音 60 分钟"定时模式）。定时静音到点后气泡提示
  "声音已恢复"。
- 不限时静音重启后保留；定时静音不跨重启。

### 可靠性

- 进球和赛果反馈改为 watcher 两段式执行：先完成本地音效与头部动作，再启动局域网
  动态 TTS。TTS 请求保持阻塞，WAV 播放期间不再每秒发出 4 次状态查询；如果仅语音
  失败，也不会把已经播过的进球庆祝重新来一遍。
- 盘口疑似进球提醒恢复“盘口突变 / 很可能进球 / 先别眨眼”的紧张感，同时继续
  明确球队，并保留等待文字直播确认的不确定性。
- TTS 播放中的音效改为成功跳过而不是报错，不再触发 watcher 的失败重试——
  这是比赛中"重试风暴"修复的设备侧一半。
- watcher 失败投递改为退避 10 秒后重试，普通实况最多重试 2 次（进球/点球/红牌
  放宽到 5 次）。此前失控的重试循环会在直播中把设备打到内存耗尽重启。
- 概率条或滚动条可见期间屏幕不再自动调暗，消除安静时段的暗/亮闪烁。
